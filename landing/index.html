Why OHD

Designed for an era where agents do the work.

Agent-native, not bolted-on

Three FastMCP servers ship on day one — Connect for personal use, Care for clinicians, Emergency for paramedics. LLMs are first-class consumers, not a sidecar.

Your data, your storage

Run storage on your phone, your home server, OHD Cloud, or a clinic operator. The protocol is identical — only the deployment topology differs.

Real, not just spec

11 form factors built: 3 web SPAs, 2 Android Compose apps, 4 CLIs, 3 Python MCP servers. 600+ tests across the project.

Open + dual-licensed

OHDC over Connect-RPC and HTTP/3. Specifications and reference implementations dual-licensed Apache-2.0 OR MIT. Build any client.

How it works

Three tiers. One protocol.

Storage holds the events. OHDC moves them. Consumers — humans or agents — read and write under grants the user issues, scopes, and revokes.

Consumers

Connect

Web, Android, CLI, MCP. Personal logging and dashboards.

Care

Clinician roster, cases, write-with-approval, two-sided audit.

Emergency

Paramedic-scoped beacon read with authority signing.

Your AI agent

Any MCP-aware client. Acts under explicit user grants.

OHDC over Connect-RPC + HTTP/3 grants · channels · audit log · revocation

Clinic operator

Self-hosted, OIDC-delegated.

Relay — NAT-bridges home instances and signs emergency-authority tokens.
  • Per-channel encryption for sensitive classes — mental health, sexual health, substance use — with separate keys.
  • Audit log on every grant ever queried. Two-sided between operators and patients.
  • Synchronous revocation. Pull a grant; the consumer's next call is denied.
  • Predecessor / parent inheritance for clinical cases. Built for real workflows.

Sideloading

  1. Open Settings → Apps → Special access → Install unknown apps, allow your browser.
  2. Tap the downloaded .apk in your Files app and confirm the install.
  3. Open OHD Connect. Point it at your storage URL and paste a grant token.

For operators

Self-host for a clinic, an EMS, or a household.

Native packages

.deb for Debian / Ubuntu, .rpm for Fedora / RHEL, PKGBUILD for Arch. Systemd units, sane defaults, log rotation.

Containers

A docker-compose.yml template ships with the repo. Production deployments use Caddy in front for TLS and HTTP/3.

Identity

Bring your own OIDC provider — Google, Okta, Authentik, Keycloak — or use the storage's built-in OAuth endpoints for small deployments.

Two-sided audit

Operators see who their staff queried; patients see who queried them. Both sides keep tamper-evident logs.
